How To Fix Data And OBB Files Not Showing

Oculus Only Way

--THIS IS A STEP BY STEP GUIDE--

--1- How to install APK files (SKIP THIS IF YOU ALREADY HAVE A FILE MANAGER LIKE CX, NP OR MT)--

Step 1: Go to Oculus Store, and search for "Mobile VR Station" and download it.

Step 2: Once you have installed it, use the trigger on your oculus (where your index is) or tap it with your actual fingers. (This is how you will Open and Tap things.)

Step 3: You will be met with a Welcome screen, Click on the red power button at the bottom

Step 4: Go to Local Files Folder then All Folders then Downloads Folder Then open the File you installed (APK file) and click "Install"

CONGRATULATIONS!

--2- How to use installed File Manager APK--

Step 1: Go to where you open your monkey games

Step 2: At the top Right corner, click on "All" and choose "Unknown Sources". You should see your file manager there

Step 3: Open the File Manager to install and manage files

--3- __How to get OBB and data folder working again__--

Step 1: Download Shizuku APK file (https://github.com/RikkaApps/Shizuku/releases)

Step 2: Download Lightning Launcher (https://github.com/threethan/LightningLauncher/releases/)

Step 3: Open BOTH Shizuku a AND Lightning Launcher, BOTH should appear infront of you

Step 4 In Lightning Launcher, Open Android settings, NOT HEADSET SETTINGS

Step 5 In Lightning Launcher Android Settings, Scroll until you see "About Device" or "About Headset"

Step 6: In Lightning Launcher Android Settings, Tap the "Build Number" like 10 times or something till it says "You are a Developer!"

Step 7: In Lightning Launcher Android Settings, Go back (Same place you found About Headset) Then scroll download to "System" then go to "Developer Option"

Step 7: In Lightning Launcher Android Settings, Scroll down to "Wireless Debugging" and Enable it

Step 8: Now if it says "Allow" or "Don't Allow" then choose "Allow". After that click on "Pair with code"

Step9: In Shizuku, Click on Pair with code thentype in the 6-digit code from Lightning Launcher

Step 10: In Shizuku, Scroll up to "authorized apps" and enable it for "NP File Manager" -->LINK TO NP FILE MANAGER APK(https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1155541913521373285/1227276104071512074/NP_Manager_3.1.3.apk?ex=6627d0f0&is=66155bf0&hm=58e4dfbd73c939455ec594da73927c75f709c33dbe84e45b1109452f32f32cee&) <-- (COPY IT AND PASTE IN URL)

CONGRATS YOU CAN NOW OPEN OBB AND DATA FOLDER, KEEP IN MIND YOU HAVE TO THIS EVERY TIME YOU POWER ON YOUR HEADSET/RESTART IT

TO ENABLE DEVELOPER SETTINGS: Go into Android Settings in Lightning Launcher Go to "About Headset" Find "Build" and TAP that 5x + times to unlock Developer Settings.
